Youth Protection Policy

Commitment: KANBEE is committed to protecting youth from harmful content and ensuring a safe online environment.
1. Protection Measures

We implement technical and administrative measures to prevent youth from accessing harmful content.

2. Reporting System

Users can report harmful content through our dedicated reporting channel. We will take prompt action to address any reported issues.

3. Education and Training

We provide regular education and training for our staff on youth protection policies and procedures.

Effective Date: 2024-01-01
